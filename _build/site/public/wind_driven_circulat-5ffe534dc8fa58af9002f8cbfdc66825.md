# Wind-driven Circulation

Consider steady, homogenous horizontal flow with friction:
:::{note} 
$$ 
-fv &= -\frac{1}{\rho_0} \partial_x p + \partial_z \tau^{(x)}  \\
fu &= -\frac{1}{\rho_0} \partial_y p + \partial_z \tau^{(y)}  
$$
:::
We can decompose $v,u$ into geostrophic and ageostrophic components. 

$$
-fv_{ag} &= \partial_z \tau^{x}  \\
fu_{ag} &= \partial_z \tau^{y}  
$$
