# Hydrological Cycle 

## Clausius-Clayperon Equation 

### Clayperon Relation 

:::{prf:theorem}
At phase boundary, the chemical potentials are equal:
\begin{equation}
\mu(\alpha, p,T)=\mu(\beta, p, T)
\end{equation}
where $\alpha,\beta$ represent different phases
:::
Consider the change in chemical potential during phase change $\alpha\to\beta$
\begin{align*}
\mu(\beta, p,T) - \mu(\alpha, p,T)&= V_mdp - S_mdT \\
V_mdp &= S_mdT \\

\end{align*}
